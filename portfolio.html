📈 VolSurface
Arbitrage-aware implied volatility surface construction from noisy real-world options data.
Python 3.9+MIT LicenseQuant FinanceActive
⚡ Why this matters

The Problem

  • Noisy option chains with missing strikes
  • Calendar & butterfly arbitrage violations
  • Unstable interpolation across tenors
  • Bad pricing and unreliable Greeks downstream

What VolSurface Does

  • Cleans raw option chains automatically
  • Detects & repairs arbitrage violations
  • Calibrates SABR, SVI, and Heston models
  • Builds smooth, arbitrage-free surfaces
⚠Without an arbitrage-free surface, downstream Greeks are unreliable and risk models break. volsurface enforces no-arbitrage constraints throughout calibration.
✨ Key features
📊

Data Cleaning

Removes stale quotes, handles bid-ask spread outliers, fills missing strikes via interpolation.

🔍

Arbitrage Detection

Checks butterfly (correctly) and calendar spread no-arbitrage conditions across all tenors.

🧮

Model Calibration

SABR, SVI, and Heston calibration with per-expiry fitting and global surface optimization.

📉

Surface Construction

Bicubic spline and RBF interpolate across the full (K, T) plane with arbitrage regularization.

📈

Visualization

3D surface plots, vol smile plots, calibration diagnostics, and arbitrage heatmaps.

⚡

Greeks Engine

Compute delta, vega, volga from the calibrated surface using finite-difference estimators.

🚀 Quick start
$ pip install volsurface
python
from volsurface.core.surface import VolSurface

vs = VolSurface(data)
vs.clean()                        # Step 1 – remove stale quotes
vs.detect_arbitrage()              # Step 2 – detect butterfly & calendar arb
vs.calibrate(model="sabr")        # Step 3 – fit SABR per expiry
vs.build(method="spline")         # Step 4 – interpolate surface
vs.plot_surface()
vs.plot_smile(expiry="2025-03-21")
📖 API reference
MethodParametersDescription
.clean()threshold=0.05Remove outlier quotes; interpolate missing strikes.
.detect_arbitrage()mode='both'Identify butterfly & calendar arb violations. Returns report.
.calibrate()model='sabr', max_expiry=TrueFit stochastic vol model to cleaned market vols.
.build()method='spline', grid=100Interpolate surface over dense (K, T) grid.
.get_vol()strike, expiryQuery implied vol at arbitrary (K, T) coordinate.
.plot_surface()cmap='viridis'Render interactive 3D surface with matplotlib.
.greeks()strike, expiry, spotCompute delta, vega, volga from calibrated surface.
