sharesth23 / volsurface
📈 VolSurface
Arbitrage-aware implied volatility surface construction from noisy real-world options data.
Python 3.9+MIT LicenseQuant FinanceActive
⚡ Why this matters

The Problem

  • Noisy option chains with missing strikes
  • Calendar & butterfly arbitrage violations
  • Unstable interpolation across tenors
  • Bad pricing and unreliable Greeks downstream

What VolSurface Does

  • Cleans raw option chains automatically
  • Detects & repairs arbitrage violations
  • Calibrates SABR, SVI, and Heston models
  • Builds smooth, arbitrage-free surfaces
⚠Without an arbitrage-free surface, downstream Greeks are unreliable and risk models break. volsurface enforces no-arbitrage constraints throughout calibration.
✨ Key features
📊

Data Cleaning

Removes stale quotes, handles bid-ask spread outliers, fills missing strikes via interpolation.

🔍

Arbitrage Detection

Checks butterfly (correctly) and calendar spread no-arbitrage conditions across all tenors.

🧮

Model Calibration

SABR, SVI, and Heston calibration with per-expiry fitting and global surface optimization.

📉

Surface Construction

Bicubic spline and RBF interpolate across the full (K, T) plane with arbitrage regularization.

📈

Visualization

3D surface plots, vol smile plots, calibration diagnostics, and arbitrage heatmaps.

⚡

Greeks Engine

Compute delta, vega, volga from the calibrated surface using finite-difference estimators.

🚀 Quick start
$ pip install volsurface
python
from volsurface.core.surface import VolSurface

vs = VolSurface(data)
vs.clean()                        # Step 1 – remove stale quotes
vs.detect_arbitrage()              # Step 2 – detect butterfly & calendar arb
vs.calibrate(model="sabr")        # Step 3 – fit SABR per expiry
vs.build(method="spline")         # Step 4 – interpolate surface
vs.plot_surface()
vs.plot_smile(expiry="2025-03-21")
📖 API reference
MethodParametersDescription
.clean()threshold=0.05Remove outlier quotes; interpolate missing strikes.
.detect_arbitrage()mode='both'Identify butterfly & calendar arb violations. Returns report.
.calibrate()model='sabr', max_expiry=TrueFit stochastic vol model to cleaned market vols.
.build()method='spline', grid=100Interpolate surface over dense (K, T) grid.
.get_vol()strike, expiryQuery implied vol at arbitrary (K, T) coordinate.
.plot_surface()cmap='viridis'Render interactive 3D surface with matplotlib.
.greeks()strike, expiry, spotCompute delta, vega, volga from calibrated surface.

📊 Dynamic Correlation & Regime Analysis
Cross-asset correlation research framework studying time-varying dependence, regime dynamics, and systemic risk decomposition via PCA.
PythonQuant FinanceMIT License
🔬 Research motivation

Modern portfolio construction relies heavily on correlation assumptions. Empirical evidence shows correlations are non-stationary, diversification benefits collapse during crises, and cross-asset relationships shift structurally after major macro events.

🎯Core question: How stable is diversification when markets transition between regimes? This project quantifies when and why diversification fails.
🌐 Asset universe
Asset ClassInstrumentRole
EquitiesS&P 500, NASDAQ, DAXRisk-on growth exposure
FXEUR/USD, USD/JPYRisk sentiment & funding flows
RatesUS 10Y Treasury YieldPolicy & discounting channel
CommoditiesGold, Crude OilInflation hedge & growth sensitivity
🧠 Methodology
🔄

Rolling Correlation

Time-indexed matrices C_t computed over rolling window W — a full time series of dependence structures.

🔥

Heatmap Visualization

Animated heatmaps reveal clustering, hedge breakdown, and crisis-driven co-movement over time.

📐

PCA / Eigen Decomp

PC1 explained variance acts as a low-dimensional proxy for systemic market-wide co-movement.

📅

Pre vs Post-COVID

Structural shift analysis comparing 2015–2019 vs 2020+ to identify persistent correlation changes.

  • Post-COVID markets exhibit persistently elevated baseline correlations

✨ Core capabilities
📐

Duration

Macaulay and Modified duration for individual bonds and full portfolios.

💹

DV01

Dollar value of a basis point — the core hedging metric for rate desks.

📊

Convexity

Second-order price sensitivity for accurate pricing beyond linear approximation.

🎛️

Scenario Simulation

Parallel shifts, steepeners, flatteners, butterfly scenarios with P&L attribution.

sharesth23 / lightswap
⚡ LightSwap
High-performance, non-custodial submarine swap service in Rust bridging Bitcoin on-chain and the Lightning Network.
RustBitcoinLightning NetworkMIT
🔍 What is a submarine swap?

A submarine swap is an atomic cross-layer exchange — trustlessly moving funds between Bitcoin's main chain (L1) and the Lightning Network (L2) without a custodian. Both legs are secured by Hash Time Lock Contracts (HTLCs): both complete atomically or both fail.

On-chain → Lightning

  • Fund Lightning channels without managing UTXOs
  • Fund Lightning wallet from cold storage
  • Atomic — zero counterparty risk

Lightning → On-chain

  • Sweep channels to on-chain address
  • Rebalance liquidity without channel close
  • Non-custodial throughout
🦀 Why Rust?
⚡

Performance

Zero-cost abstractions for high-throughput swap routing with no GC pauses.

🔒

Memory Safety

Borrow checker eliminates entire classes of bugs critical in financial systems.

🌐

Async Runtime

Tokio-based async for concurrent HTLC state machine management.

🧩

Crate Ecosystem

bitcoin, lightning-dev, secp256k1 — best-in-class Bitcoin primitives.

🔑 Core protocol
rust
// Initiate submarine swap
let swap = SubmarineSwap::new(
    amount_sats,
    preimage_hash,
    locktime,
)?;
// Verify HTLC on-chain before paying invoice
swap.verify_htlc_confirmed(&chain_client).await?;
swap.pay_lightning_invoice(&ln_client).await?;
